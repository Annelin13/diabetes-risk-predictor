About Us

AI-Powered Diabetes Risk Predictor

Our AI-powered Diabetes Risk Predictor is a web-based tool designed to make early diabetes risk screening more accessible. Using machine learning algorithms trained on clinical datasets, it analyzes vital parameters to provide accurate risk assessments.

How It Works

The system analyzes your:

  • Glucose levels
  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Blood pressure
  • Family history
  • Other key risk factors

Using these inputs, our AI model calculates your personalized diabetes risk score.

Our Mission

This project is inspired by research from the MDPI paper on "Predicting Risk and Complications of Diabetes Through Built-in Artificial Intelligence." Our goal is to empower individuals with easy-to-understand, actionable insights about their health.
